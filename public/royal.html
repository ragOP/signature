FAQ

How long does it take to get my signature?
Delivery time depends on your chosen plan. Basic plans take 2 days, Professional takes 1 day, and Legacy plans are delivered within 24 hours.
Can I request specific styles?
Absolutely! Our calligraphers work with you to create a signature that matches your preferred style, whether it's elegant, bold, modern, or traditional.
What file formats do I receive?
You'll receive your signature in multiple formats: PNG and JPG for basic plans, with SVG and AI formats included in higher-tier plans for maximum versatility.
What if I don't like the designs?
We offer unlimited revisions on Professional and Legacy plans, and at least one revision on Basic plans. We work until you're completely satisfied.
Is my information secure?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and securely stored. We never share your details with third parties.
